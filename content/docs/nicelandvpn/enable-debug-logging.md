## Step 1 Getting into the settings
Select Settings from the sidebar.

## Step 2 Turning on/off debug and info logging
On the Configurations card, click on the off next to Debug Logging and Info Logging to turn them on and vice versa.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/info_debug_logging.png)

## Step 3 Saving your changes
Click on the SAVE button that appeared at the top to save your changes.
