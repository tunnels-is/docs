## Step 1: Downloading Niceland
Download Niceland VPN by clicking [this link](https://www.nicelandvpn.is/#/download)

## Step 2: Opening Niceland
On Windows and Linux, you can simply run Niceland! No install needed.
On MacOS, you will need to drag the application to the applications folder.

## Step 3: Creating Your Account
Accounts are created inside the Niceland VPN app, not on the website. This is for security reasons.

## Step 4: Choosing A Subscription
Detailed subscription information can be seen by clicking [this link](https://www.nicelandvpn.is/#/signup)

## Step 5: Activating Your License Key
Once payment is complete, you will receive a License key.
You can use this key inside the 'Settings' page in the app to activate your account.

![lkh](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/lkh.png)

## Step 6: Connecting To Niceland
Click a country of your choosing and select yes!
