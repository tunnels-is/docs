## Step 1 Getting into the DNS settings
Select DNS from the sidebar.

## Step 2 Changing the DNS
On the Settings card, click on the field next to Primary delete the old IP and enter the DNS IP you want. Do the same for the Secondary.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/changing_dns.png)

## Step 3 Saving your changes
Don't forget to click the SAVE button that appeared at the top to save your settings.
