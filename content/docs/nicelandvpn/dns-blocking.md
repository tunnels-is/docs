## Step 1 Getting into the DNS settings
Select DNS from the sidebar.

## Step 2 Turning on/off block-lists
On the Blocking card you can see all the categories you can block. Click on those you want to block to turn them off, click on them again to unblock them.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/dns_blocking.png)

## Step 3 Saving your changes
Click the SAVE button that appeared on the top to save your configuration.
