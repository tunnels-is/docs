## Step 1 Log-in screen
Select Login from the sidebar if you are not already there.

## Step 2 Starting the password recovery
Click on Reset Password at the bottom.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/reset_password.png)

## Step 3 Entering your email
Your account's email should be in the first field (1), if not enter it now.

## Step 4 Getting the recovery code
Press the Click To Reset Code button (2). You will receive a recovery code in your email.
Enter the recovery code in the fourth field on the form (3).

## Step 6 Choose a new password
Then enter a new password in the second and third field (4).

## Step 7 Complete the recovery
Finaly click the Reset Password button to complete the reset (5). After that you can login with your new password.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/reset_password-2.png)
