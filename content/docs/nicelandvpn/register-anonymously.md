## Step 1 Getting your token
On the login screen, click on Register Anonymously. On the first field in the form, a token will appear. Save it somewhere or you will not be able to login. This acts like your username.

## Step 2 Adding and confirming your password
Add your password in the second field and once again confirm your password in the third. Optionally you can add an affiliate code in the last field.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/registering_anonymously.png)

Click the Register button to complete the process.
