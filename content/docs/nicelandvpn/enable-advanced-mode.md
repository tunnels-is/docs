## Step 1 Getting into the settings
Select Settings from the sidebar.

## Step 2 Turning on/off advanced mode
On the Configurations card, click on the off next to Advanced Mode to turn it on and vice versa.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/advanced_mode.png)

## Step 3 Save your changes
Do not forget to click the SAVE button that appeared at the top to save your changes.
