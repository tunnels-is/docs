## Step 1 Getting into the settings
Select Settings from the sidebar.

## Step 2 Starting the 2-FA procedure
On the Account card click the Two-Factor Authentication button.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/2fa-1.png)

## Step 3 Scanning the QR code and confirming it
Scan the QR code with an authenticator App, like Google Authenticator or Aegis.
Below the QR code there is a form. You have to put the code from the authenticator App in the first field and your password in the second field. The third field is optional and can be ignored for now.
Then click confirm to complete the process.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tunnels-is/media/master/nl-website/v2/sections/how-to-use-niceland/2fa-2.png)
